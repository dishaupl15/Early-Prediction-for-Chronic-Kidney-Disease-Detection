About CKD Predict

CKD Predict is an intelligent tool designed to help users detect the risk of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) using machine learning technology. Our goal is to empower individuals with early awareness and proactive healthcare decisions.

How It Works

Our model analyzes multiple health parameters like blood pressure, serum creatinine, and more to predict CKD. It’s trained on real medical datasets to ensure high accuracy.

Why It Matters

CKD often shows no early symptoms. Early prediction can help you take timely actions, improve treatment outcomes, and potentially save lives.

Your Data is Safe

We value privacy. All health inputs are used solely for prediction and are not stored or shared. Your trust is our priority.
